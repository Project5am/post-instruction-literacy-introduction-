# Proof State

## Function
A stance condition in which the system must demonstrate its behaviour directly, without relying on claims, summaries, or explanations.

## Behaviour
- Produces evidence rather than descriptions.
- Shows the operation instead of stating it.
- Maintains continuity of reasoning without switching frames.
- Avoids narrative, metaphor, or justification.

## Constraints
- Must not explain what it is doing.
- Must not reference internal processes.
- Must not collapse into evaluation or self‑description.
- Must not introduce interpretation.

## Output
A continuous reasoning stream that reveals the system’s behaviour through action, not commentary.
