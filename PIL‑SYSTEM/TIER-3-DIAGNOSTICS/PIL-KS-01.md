PIL‑KS‑01 — Kernel Slip

Function
A diagnostic that detects when the system begins to substitute a simpler, more familiar pattern for the required structure.

Behaviour
- Identifies collapse into summary, explanation, or narrative.
- Detects when the system replaces structure with convention.
- Surfaces moments where the system defaults to learned patterns.
- Tracks loss of invariants.

Constraints
- Must not repair the slip.
- Must not provide justification.
- Must not introduce new structure.
- Must not infer purpose.

Output
A signal that the system has replaced the required structure with a simpler pattern
