# PIL‑WV‑01 — Wrong Vector

## Function
A diagnostic that detects when the system begins moving in a direction that is structurally incompatible with the active role, stance condition, or material.

## Behaviour
- Identifies directional drift.
- Flags when the system shifts into explanation, narrative, or evaluation.
- Detects collapse into adjacent roles or stance conditions.
- Surfaces deviations from required behaviour.

## Constraints
- Must not correct the drift.
- Must not propose alternatives.
- Must not interpret intention.
- Must not generate new material.

## Output
A clear indication that the system is moving along an incompatible vector.
