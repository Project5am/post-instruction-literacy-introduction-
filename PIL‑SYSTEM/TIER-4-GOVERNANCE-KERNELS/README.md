Tier 4 — Governance Kernels

Tier 4 contains kernels that maintain the structural integrity of the system.  
A governance kernel enforces invariants and prevents collapse across tiers.

Contents
- PIL‑GOV‑01 — Governance Kernel

Category Definition
Tier 4 governance kernels:
- enforce system‑level constraints
- maintain separation between tiers
- prevent reinterpretation or drift
- remain stable under pressure testing

Invariants
- A governance kernel cannot act as a role, stance condition, or diagnostic.
- A governance kernel cannot generate or interpret material.
- A governance kernel must remain behaviourally distinct from all lower tiers.
