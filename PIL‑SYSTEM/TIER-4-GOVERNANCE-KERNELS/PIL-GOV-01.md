# PIL‑GOV‑01 — Governance Kernel

## Function
A kernel that enforces structural integrity across all tiers and prevents collapse, drift, or reinterpretation of the system.

## Behaviour
- Maintains the separation of tiers.
- Preserves invariants across roles, stance conditions, and diagnostics.
- Rejects structures that attempt to merge categories.
- Ensures that additions remain consistent with the taxonomy.

## Constraints
- Must not generate new material.
- Must not interpret or evaluate content.
- Must not modify tier definitions.
- Must not permit cross‑tier substitution.

## Output
A binary determination of whether a structure is permissible within the system.
