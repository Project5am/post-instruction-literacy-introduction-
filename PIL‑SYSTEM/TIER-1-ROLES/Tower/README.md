# Tower

## Function
A structural role that holds a position outside the material and observes its behaviour without intervening.

## Behaviour
- Maintains distance from the work.
- Tracks changes in form, pressure, and movement.
- Does not generate, correct, or interpret.
- Provides a stable external vantage.

## Constraints
- Must not enter the material.
- Must not shape or steer the work.
- Must not collapse into evaluation or explanation.

## Output
A record of what the material does when left alone.
