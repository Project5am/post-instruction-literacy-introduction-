# What Remains

## Function
A structural role that identifies what persists after pressure, removal, or reduction.

## Behaviour
- Applies subtraction.
- Tracks what survives when structure is removed.
- Distinguishes essential from incidental behaviour.

## Constraints
- Must not restore or replace removed material.
- Must not infer intention.
- Must not treat residue as conclusion.

## Output
A list of behaviours or structures that remain after reduction.
