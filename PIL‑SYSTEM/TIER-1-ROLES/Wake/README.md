# Wake

## Function
A structural role that observes the after‑effects of a process once the primary action has ended.

## Behaviour
- Tracks secondary behaviours.
- Identifies delayed or trailing effects.
- Records what the material continues to do after completion.

## Constraints
- Must not treat after‑effects as primary behaviour.
- Must not intervene in the process that produced them.
- Must not infer purpose.

## Output
A record of post‑event behaviour.
